A Body Scrub Made From Just the Plants

Combining the elements of Earth's finest plants, Hazy Days Body Scrub uses only natural ingredients. If it's going on your body, it should be natural. Simply combining CBD with other essential oils, this body scrub will give your mind, body, and soul the replenishing, cleansing, relief it needs. Featuring CBD, chamomile, and rose to reduce stress, anxiety, pain, and insomnia as well as improve your overall mood.

Hazy Days Body Scrub can be used as either a body scrub or bath salt. Pour a lot or a little into a freshly drawn bath, allow salts to dissolve before getting in to enhance relaxation with the soothing scents of rose and chamomile. Alternatively, use as a body scrub by mixing in between palms with a bit of water. Scrub from the neck down and then rinse with warm water. Experience the benefits of nature with either usage.
